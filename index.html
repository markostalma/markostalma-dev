ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Marko Štalmatović! I am a Web developer and designer based in Belgrade, Serbia. Have several years of experience in various spheres of design and programming. I am currently working as a Web Designer and Developer in the Netops corporation where is my passion for design and programming is merged.

Besides working as a graphic designer in a variety of companies and as freelance, during my studies, I also got interested in web design. Ever since I was a child, I was keen on new technologies, and over time my desire for learning to program grew. Knowing HTML & CSS, as well as Basic Javascript / jQuery, I took a step further. I enrolled Master studies at the Information Technology Chair at Singidunum University. In the meantime, I have been admitted to a course where I have learned a lot about web programming in PHP programming language and Drupal CMS. At the moment I am doing PhD studies on Intelligent Systems at the University of Belgrade.
